HexScore Algorithm

Eight-Dimensional Player Valuation Engine (0–100)

The Eight Dimensions

Core valuation axes, ordered by weight. All eight dimensions weighted (sum 1.00).

Full Formula

Complete equation breakdown — every weight, every conditional, and how they compose into the final 0–100 score.

Part A — Weighted Composite
rawComposite = always
  (Production × 0.24) 4-yr weighted avg
+ (Situation × 0.16) team context & scheme
+ (Scarcity × 0.14) positional rank percentile
+ (Durability × 0.12) GP ratio + injury dampener
+ (Consistency × 0.10) availability & production CV
+ (Health × 0.08) current injury status
+ (Age Factor × 0.08) position career arc
+ (Slot Value × 0.08) roster economics
All 8 weights: 0.24 + 0.16 + 0.14 + 0.12 + 0.10 + 0.08 + 0.08 + 0.08 = 1.00
Part B — Archetype Adjustments
if archetype has fantasyPremium: conditional
  adjusted.Production ×= fantasyPremium e.g. elite pass-catchers
if archetype has floorBoost: conditional
  adjusted.Consistency += floorBoost e.g. bellcow, workhorse
Part C — Format Multiplier (PPR / Half / Standard)
formatMult = 1 + (formatWeight × receptionIndex × posCoeff) data-driven, not flat
  formatWeight: 0 (Std) | 0.5 (Half) | 1.0 (PPR) league scoring format
  receptionIndex: norm(tgt/gm, catchRate, airYdShare) player-specific volume
  posCoeff: historical Std→PPR scoring differential position-level calibration
posCoeff reference (avg PPG delta, Full PPR vs Standard):
  RB: +3.8 – +8.2 ppg widest variance — pass-catching vs. grinder
  TE: +2.4 – +5.6 ppg receiving TE vs. blocking TE
  WR: +3.2 – +5.0 ppg slot/possession vs. deep threat
  QB: +0.0 ppg no reception-based scoring impact
Part D — Modifiers
modified = adjusted multiplicative chain
  × posWeight position scoring-band compression
  × formatMult from Part C above
  × xFactor(usageShare) range 0.85 – 1.10
  × draftValue(adpRank) range 0.05 – 1.00
posWeight: QB = 1.00, RB = 1.00, WR = 1.00, TE = 1.00 full range positions
posWeight: K = 0.72, DEF = 0.68 compressed — ceiling ~67 / ~63
Part E — Sigmoid + Elite Floor
shaped = 1 / (1 + e−8(modified − 0.40)) × 100 always — maps to 0–100
final = clamp(shaped, eliteFloor) always
eliteFloor = peakScore × (0.85 ^ yearsSincePeak) historical peak decay 15%/yr
